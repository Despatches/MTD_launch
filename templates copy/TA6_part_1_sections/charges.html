12. Charges

12.1 Do you have to pay any charges relating to the property (apart from council tax, utility charges, and so on)?

Yes

No

Give details.
For example, if your property is freehold, charges might include payments to a management company or residents’ association, or for a private drainage system.
If your property is leasehold, you’ll need to give details of lease expenses (such as service charges and ground rent). Your solicitor will provide you with a TA7 once the preliminary.
