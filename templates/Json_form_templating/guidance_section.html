Guidance



The TA6 part 1 covers many of the basic elements of disclosure providing a stable base for buyers to finalise negotiations and for a conveyancer to begin the disclosure process and comply Law Society & other regulated standards.

The TA6 part (1) one can also be used as a start point for the Expansive Cascade Disclosurewhich can also be filled out independantly from the TA6 forms


Throughout the form you are free to leave any question blank if you so choose and return to it at a later date.If you are unsure of the correct answer leave the question blank until you can find out the required information, or seek the required guidance/ assistance.

You may remove / delete any input data or deselect any selection by using the (yellow) "X" button normally located on the right of a given answer.
After completing each section press the button to move on to the next section (you can return to any section you have already submitted data for (or left empty) at anytime)

Note that in some places an answer segement of the form may need to be filled out multiple times, for example sets of relevant addresses or details of relevant persons
